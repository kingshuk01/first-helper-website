First Helpers

We have launched a two-wheeler ambulance service called ‘RESCUE' to reach essential medical care during the ‘golden hour’ post accidents or Incidents. A Two-wheeler ambulance service will be launched, promising to cut time for patients needing instant response.


Equipped with life-saving drugs, oxygen cylinders and trained paramedic drivers, the motorbike ambulances are meant to provide emergency medical care to patients till a fully equipped ambulance arrives. We Promise 5 minutes is all that will be needed for the two-wheeler ambulances to reach a patient in need.
